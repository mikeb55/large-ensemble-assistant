# Excellence Evaluation Criteria
Version: 2025.12.12

## Target Score: ≥ 8.5 / 10

If any output scores below 8.5, automatically generate a refined version.
Do NOT stop until the target is reached.

---

## Primary Excellence Categories (10 points each)

### 1. Melodic Identity (10)
- Does the melody have a distinctive, memorable shape?
- Is there a clear motivic "fingerprint"?
- Can you hum it after one listen?

### 2. Melodic Development (10)
- Does the melody evolve throughout the piece?
- Are there variations, inversions, augmentations?
- Does it avoid literal repetition without variation?

### 3. Harmonic Depth (10)
- Are the harmonies sophisticated and colorful?
- Is there functional logic or intentional non-functionality?
- Are tensions and extensions used musically (not randomly)?

### 4. Rhythm & Groove (10)
- Is the rhythmic language interesting and varied?
- Does it have a human feel (not quantized/mechanical)?
- Are there syncopations, polyrhythms, metric interest?

### 5. Pacing & Breath (10)
- Are there natural breathing points?
- Does the pacing feel organic (not rushed or stagnant)?
- Is there tension and release in phrase structure?

### 6. Texture Balance (10)
- Is there variety in texture (thick/thin, tutti/solo)?
- Are voices well-spaced vertically?
- Does the texture serve the musical content?

### 7. Orchestration Logic (10)
- Are parts idiomatic for each instrument?
- Is there register awareness and balance?
- Are timbral choices intentional and effective?

### 8. Form & Structure (10)
- Is there a clear formal logic?
- Are sections distinct yet related?
- Does the piece have architectural coherence?

### 9. Human Expressivity (10)
- Does it feel like a human wrote and would perform it?
- Are there dynamic nuances, agogic accents?
- Is there emotional authenticity?

### 10. Afterglow / Memorability (10)
- Does the piece leave a lasting impression?
- Would you want to hear it again?
- Does it resonate emotionally after it ends?

## Secondary Metrics

### Humanisation Score (Target: ≥ 8.5)
- Phrase shapes: lift → arc → release
- Breath marks and natural rests
- Realistic dynamics (not mechanical)
- Slightly irregular rhythms (human feel)
- No quantized AI-patterns

### Contrast Score (Target: ≥ 8.0)
- Dynamic contrast (pp to ff range)
- Registral contrast (high vs. low)
- Textural contrast (dense vs. sparse)
- Rhythmic contrast (active vs. sustained)
- Timbral contrast (varied orchestration)

### Engraving Accuracy (Target: ≥ 8.5)
- No collisions (dynamics, slurs, articulations)
- Clean vertical spacing
- Proper clef usage
- Readable system breaks
- Correct copyright placement
