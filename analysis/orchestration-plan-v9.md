### Orchestration Details

**m.1-2: Cello Pedal**
- **Cello:** Low C2 pedal, growling (sul ponticello, slight vibrato)
- **Double Bass:** C1 pedal (pizz on beat 1, then arco sustain)
- **Guitar:** Cm7 voicing (C-G-Bb-Eb), arpeggiated, pp
- **Others:** Tacet

**m.3: V9 Bass Poetry**
- **Cello:** C3-Eb3-D3 lyrical gesture (SOLO, singing, independent)
- **Guitar:** F7 sustained pad
- **Viola:** Enters beat 5 with Ab3 color tone (pppp, emerging)

**m.4-5: Flugelhorn Entry (V9 Long Slur Arc)**
- **Flugelhorn:** Lead melody C5-Eb5-F5-G5... (mf, singing)
- **Clarinet:** Shadow line, 3rd below or color tones
- **Viola:** Countermelody (contrary motion fragments)
- **Cello:** Walking quarter notes (C-G-C-Bb pattern)
- **Guitar:** Cm7 sustained, gentle arpeggios
- **Double Bass:** Root motion (half notes)

**m.6-9: Development**
- **Flugelhorn:** Continues lead
- **Violin:** Enters m.7, doubling melody 8va (color)
- **Flute:** Color tones on held notes (add 9ths, 11ths)
- **Viola/Cello:** Counterlines in 3rds/6ths
- **Guitar:** More active harmonic rhythm, chord changes

**m.10-11: Climax Build (V9 Rhythmic Honesty)**

- **Flugelhorn:** Harmony line (3rd below)
- **Clarinet:** Upper extension (doubles Vln 8va)
- **All strings:** Building density, staggered entries
- **Guitar:** Fuller voicings, strummed
- **Double Bass:** Strong root motion

**m.12: Transition (V9 Phrase Messiness)**
- **Flugelhorn:** Bb5-Ab5-G5-F5-Eb5 descending
- **Violin:** C5 early entry beat 6, F#5 anticipation beat 7
- **Others:** Rapid diminuendo, clearing for Mvt II

## Wheeler/Schneider-Inspired Colors

### Timbral Techniques
- **Shared breathing:** Instruments enter/exit together in phrases
- **Overlap handoffs:** Notes pass between instruments with 1-2 beat overlap
- **Register trading:** Same pitch moves between timbres
- **Dynamic matching:** Careful balance, no instrument dominates

### Harmonic Approach
- **Extensions:** Use 9ths, 11ths, 13ths freely
- **Voicing transparency:** Open spacings, avoid muddy middle
- **Color chords:** EbMaj7, BbMaj7/C slash chords
- **Bass independence:** Bass poetry moments throughout

### Orchestration Density Guide
| Level | Instruments Active | Character |
|-------|-------------------|-----------|
| Sparse | 1-2 | Exposed, intimate |
| Medium | 3-4 | Chamber dialogue |
| Full | 5-6 | Rich, building |
| Tutti | 7-8 | Climactic, unified |
